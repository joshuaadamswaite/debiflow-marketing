How it works

A repeatable workflow that turns portfolio data into investable exposure and keeps settlement inside agreed controls.

1) Data intake
Validate schema, formats, and required fields.
2) Investor allocation
Allocate receivables across investors for security sharing.
3) Payment in
Calculate disbursement amounts per investor.
4) Repayments upload
Ingest repayment data for allocated receivables.
5) Allocation and repurchases
Map payments to receivables, apply repurchases, set interest dates.
6) Payment out
Bridge settlement cadence with limits and covenants.

Protections and enforceable controls

Receivable-backed funding protected by limits, repurchases, early warnings, and enforceable security.

Funding and collateral
  • Finance only verified, already issued receivables
  • Limit the funded share of the portfolio
  • Advance rates preserve headroom for interest and repurchases
  • Growth limits aligned to forward capital requirements
Risk limits and repurchases
  • Covenants for risk, ageing, and concentration
  • Repurchase controls keep key metrics within thresholds
  • Receivables repurchased before a defined DPD ceiling
Legal and oversight
  • Standardised documentation defines timing and waterfalls
  • Collections account pledge supports rapid enforcement
  • Ongoing visibility into performance and collections activity
