How it works

A repeatable workflow that turns a loan tape into investable exposure, then keeps settlement inside agreed controls.

1) Upload the loan tape
Validate fields, formats, and eligibility rules.
2) Investor allocation
Allocate receivables across mandates for clean security sharing.
3) Draw and fund
Generate investor drawdown notices and disburse per unit.
4) Repayments upload
Ingest repayments and keep the register in sync.
5) Allocation and repurchases
Map payments to receivables, flag exceptions, apply repurchases.
6) Settle and report
Produce statements and settle on monthly, quarterly, or term cadence.

Protections and enforceable controls

Receivable-backed funding protected by limits, repurchases, early warnings, and enforceable security.

Funding and collateral
  • Finance only verified, already-issued receivables
  • Receivable-only collateral, with clear eligibility rules
  • Advance rates preserve headroom for interest and repurchases
  • Concentration and growth limits per mandate
Risk limits and repurchases
  • Covenants for risk, ageing, and concentration
  • Repurchase controls keep key metrics within thresholds
  • DPD caps, early triggers, and exception queues
Legal and oversight
  • Standardised documentation defines timing and waterfalls
  • Collections account pledge (bank cession) supports enforcement
  • Debt registers and investor statements, reconciled to bank activity
